🐴 ABOUT THE BEAST

This is a 1992 Ford Bronco XLT — Oxford White over Dark Blue Two-Tone, original blue tweed interior — that I have driven as my daily driver in Austin for eight years. Bought it in early 2018 at roughly 226,000 miles; it is now at approximately 261,000. I am not a "car guy." I am someone who found a great truck, drove it seriously, and kept it properly maintained.

💰 Over $24,000 in documented service receipts since purchase — all available for the buyer.

WHAT IT HAD WHEN I BOUGHT IT (2018):

  • Engine: New timing chain. Rebuilt fuel injectors. All-new engine sensors.
  • Cooling & Climate: New radiator and water pump. AC system rebuilt — ran ice cold at purchase.
  • Suspension & Steering: Six new shocks including the dual front shock setup. Rebuilt steering column. New OEM Ford steering wheel.
  • Body & Paint: Repainted in original Oxford White and Dark Blue prior to sale.
  • Interior: Original blue tweed — crack-free dash, no tears or significant wear on the seat fabric. Remarkably well-preserved for a 33-year-old truck.

Condition: Drives GREAT!! 4WD is excellent — got me out of the 2021 Austin snowpocalypse without hesitation!! AC blows cold. Interior is in great shape for a 33-year-old truck!!

Known Issues: Some exterior trim pieces missing. Paint has cosmetic wear — scratches and minor dings consistent with eight years as a daily driver, nothing structural. Tailgate latch not functioning. Hub covers on hand but not installed.

What Comes With It: Running boards, aftermarket sound system, phone/dash mount, hub covers, XLT 2" tow package, underbody rock guards, 31" tires, and the full service file.
