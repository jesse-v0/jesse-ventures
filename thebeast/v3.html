SELLER DESCRIPTION

This is a 1992 Ford Bronco XLT — Oxford White over Dark Blue Two-Tone, original blue tweed interior — that I have driven as my daily in Austin for eight years. Bought in early 2018 at roughly 226,000 miles; now at approximately 261,000. I am not a car guy. I am someone who found a great truck, drove it seriously, and kept it properly maintained. Over $24,000 in documented service receipts since purchase — all available for the buyer.

What It Had When I Bought It (2018)

The truck was substantially refreshed by the seller before purchase:

  • Engine: New timing chain. Rebuilt fuel injectors. All-new engine sensors.
  • Cooling & Climate: New radiator and water pump. AC system rebuilt.
  • Suspension & Steering: Six new shocks including the dual front shock setup. Rebuilt steering column. New OEM Ford steering wheel.
  • Body & Paint: Repainted in original Oxford White and Dark Blue prior to sale.
  • Interior: Original blue tweed — crack-free dash, no tears or significant wear.
Mechanical Work Since 2018

Engine & Ignition:

  • 2019 — Starter motor replaced; coolant temperature sensor replaced.
  • 2020 — Full tune-up: distributor, ignition coil assembly, rotor, spark plugs.
  • 2024 — Water pump, thermostat, radiator hoses; fuel injection service.
  • 2025 — Cylinder heads replaced with machine shop work. Timing cover assembly replaced. All 8 spark plugs and wires replaced. Major top-end overhaul.

Condition

Drives great. 4WD is excellent — got me out of the 2021 Austin snowpocalypse without hesitation and has worked reliably on multiple job sites. AC blows cold. Interior is in great shape for a 33-year-old truck.
